Easy Vegetarian Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • 1/2 Tomato
  • 1 Avocado
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 Cups Romaine Lettuce
  • 1 Tsp Pepper
  • 1/2 Tsp Salta

Directions

  1. Place Olive Oil, Salt, and Pepper in a Salad Bowl
  2. Add Lettuce and toss
  3. Once lettuce is completely covered, add the rest of the ingredients
  4. Toss and serve!
