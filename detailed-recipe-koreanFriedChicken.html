Korean Fried Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs (at room temperature)
  • 1 Quart Buttermilk
  • 2 Quarts Peanut Oil
  • 3 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Tbsp Pepper
  • 2lb Bone-In Chicken Drumsticks

Directions

  1. Mix Buttermilk, eggs, salt, and pepper
  2. Soak chicken in your buttermilk combination
  3. Remove Chicken and thoroughly bread
  4. Fry at 375 Degrees for 3 minutes
  5. Remove chicken, after 10 minutes, fry for another 3 minutes
  6. Rest until cool and serve!
